About Us

The world population is a rapidly growing factor. Meanwhile maintaining the balance between the demand and supply of essential food items has become critical. Rice is one of the main type of nutrient supplying foods in Sri Lanka. So, keeping the sustainable gap between demand and the supply of paddy is essential.

Current systems for this predictions on future demand and supply are two independent methods, and the impact of background factors are not monitored together to have an exact predicted value.

So, we are suggesting a single platform, where we would consider numerous factors affecting on the amount of crop yield by the next harvesting season, and the factors affecting on future demand for rice around Sri Lanka.

Considering these both values we may come up with a predicted crop yield and the predicted demand on district base by next harvesting season.

We are suggesting a single platform to monitor the impact of background factors on paddy crop yield and the rice demand.

Based on these results we will be implement a module to predict the price of paddy/ kg and the amount of rice needed to be exported in order to fulfill the demand.

And also based on the predicted crop yield, we will be implementing a schedule to distribute the available paddy harvest in an optimized manner around the country.
